About Us

Photography Club IIT Kanpur is a place where people who have an affection for photography can exchange their views, perspective and works. Here the work of the whole campus community can be enjoyed, acknowledged, and new ideas can be incurred and executed.

There is always something new to learn and explore photography. Practising new tricks, techniques to make photographs more scintillating and frames more appealing to eyes and advancing these ideas is our primary motive.

The club's studio is our home where we can exercise our creativity with all the setting according to our plan. Here with the aid of several props and light setup, we can create a photograph on a particular idea.

DTo promote photography amidst the campus community, we arrange a summer workshop named PIXELS in which we also have a professional photographer to guide our ways. Along with this, we organise photo walks, studio nights, open-house and numerous workshops which helps people to explore their fields of interests. We also set up an exhibition to showcase consolidated works of the club in front of the large audience consisting of the new batch of IITK and students of more than 200 different colleges coming to IITK in our college cultural fest Antaragni. Keep capturing life and creating art.
